Activities

University of Cincinnati Marching Band

I've been a part of the University of Cincinnati's Bearcat Bands since the Fall of 2016 as a part of the Color Guard. In 2018 I became the Color Guard Captain.

Tau Beta Sigma

In the Spring of 2017 I became an active member of Tau Beta Sigma, a greek organization dedicated to serving college bands. In the Spring of 2018 I also became the chapters Secretary, which places me in charge of the relations from the chapter as a whole to the band and in charge of the band's merchandise.

The Christ Hospital

Since the Fall of 2016, every class semester I've volunteered at the Christ Hospital. I've been a desk attendant, twice an administrative aid, and lastly an assistant to the Continuing Education department.
